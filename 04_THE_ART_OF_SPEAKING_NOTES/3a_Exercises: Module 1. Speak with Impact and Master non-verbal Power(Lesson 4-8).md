## Day 1: The Foundation - Power Pose
* **Goal:** To build the muscle memory for a confident physical stance.
* **15-Min Action Plan:**
    1.  **Find Your Base:** Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, balancing your weight evenly.
    2.  **The Stride:** Take a small, comfortable step forward with one foot.
    3.  **The Posture:** Imagine a string pulling your head up, straightening your spine. Let your shoulders relax down and back.
    4.  **The Lean:** Lean your entire upper body just slightly forward from the hips to show engagement.
    5.  **The Drill:** Hold this pose for 2-3 minutes, then relax for 30 seconds. Repeat this cycle for the full 15-minute session.
